Usage: clock [-h] [-c=color] [--version] [--help] [--hide-exit-text]

clock is an ASCII art clock designed to be a part of a terminal multiplex setup.
The clock displayed is always printed to the center of a window. Keep in 
mind that the clock can be up to 1 second off and the time displayed is that
of your local timezone. Different timezones are to be added at a later date.

commands:
	-h	--help		print this information
	-c [color]		set clock to different color)
	   red
	   green
	   yellow
	   blue
	   magenta
	   cyan
           white (default)

	--version		print current version (0.9.1)
	--hide-exit-text	hide text info at the bottom of the screen
